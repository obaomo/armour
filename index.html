The Endorsements

Quality Horse Stalls

Armour horse stalls and gates are assembled by skilled tradesmen and use the highest quality aluminum materials available on the American metal market. The advantage of using aluminum metals in the fabrication of our aluminum horse stalls is to ensure a quality product that does not rust or need replacing! Armour horse stalls arrive assembled WITH all the necessary hardware for installation, NO hardware kits to purchase and NO product assembly upon arrival! Our horse stalls for sale are simple and easy to install and arrive with detailed installation instructions and 24 hour technical advice. If you are interested in spending more time with your horses and less time in the barn maintaining your stalls, then Armour's horse stall products are for you!

Affordable Horse Stalls

Our eco friendly, reusable, aluminum door frames will outlast their iron counterpart and have less impact on the environment, over the long run keeping your cost on horse stable components low. Armour will manufacture custom length horse stall fronts, partitions, divider and window grilles, upon request, at no additional cost. The sliding door hardware includes easy slide round track and ball bearing wheels, aluminum door stops, latch and door guides arrive with each stall door, so no additional hardware kit is necessary to purchase. Whether you are looking to purchase one horse gate or multiple horse stall fronts, whether you own Andalusians or miniatures, Armour can assist in finding a perfect and affordable horse stall system for you.

Innovative & Stylish Horse Stalls

Armour's horse stalls for sale are unique in appearance and are sure to appeal to the most discriminating equine enthusiasts or barn contractors. Our product line is extensive with over 85 different style horse stall doors and gates in both sliding, hinged, interior and exterior. Features range from a straight to fold down with gossip and lift out types in between. Door bottom assemblies include all lumber, all aluminum or lumber and aluminum combination's. Our CNC capability increases the diversity of our horse stall doors and allows us to create a unique equine stalls system for any stable. Armour has pioneered the "concealed weld" technique and uses it throughout the horse stall product line which ensures the welded connection to be uncluttered and neat in appearance!
